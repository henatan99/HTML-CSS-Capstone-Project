GLOBAL TOUR CONCERT FOR WORLD PEACE 2020

The global music tour for peace aims at using music as a powerful instrument to break the wall between the peoples and nations of the world and to share love, peace and unity. We will hold hands and, sing and celebrate for peace. Highly acclaimed international and local artists will lighten up the concert.
